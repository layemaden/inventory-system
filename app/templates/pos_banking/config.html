How Charges Work

  • Total Amount Entry: You enter the total amount (transaction + charge combined). The system extracts your profit automatically.
  • Withdrawal: Customer pays Total. You give them (Total - Charge) in cash. Charge is your profit.
  • Deposit: Customer gives you Total in cash. You transfer (Total - Charge) to their account. Charge is your profit.
  • Tiers: Create multiple tiers for different amount ranges (e.g., N0-10,000 = N100 charge, N10,001-50,000 = N200 charge)
  • Percentage: Enter value like 1.5 for 1.5% of the transaction amount
