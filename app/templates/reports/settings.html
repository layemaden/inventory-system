POS Transaction Fee

Configure the fee charged by the bank for POS sales transactions. The fee is calculated per transaction as a percentage of each sale amount, capped at a maximum value per transaction.

Enter the percentage (e.g., 0.5 for 0.5%, 1.5 for 1.5%)

Maximum fee amount regardless of percentage calculation

Example Calculation (Per Transaction)

If fee = 0.5% (cap N100):

Sale 1: N50,000 × 0.5% = N250 → capped at N100
Sale 2: N10,000 × 0.5% = N50 → N50
Sale 3: N30,000 × 0.5% = N150 → capped at N100

Total daily fee = N100 + N50 + N100 = N250
