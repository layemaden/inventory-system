How Balances Are Calculated

Cash Balance =

Cash Sales + Deposit Total - Withdrawal Amount + Previous Day's Cash Carryover

Withdrawal: Cash Out = Amount (total - fee). Fee goes to POS.

Deposit: Cash In = Total (full amount received from customer).

Carryover: Uses the day before the report start date.

POS Balance =

POS Sales - POS Fee + Withdrawal Total - Deposit Amount + Previous Day's POS Carryover

POS Fee: {{ pos_fee_percentage }}% of POS sales, capped at N{{ "{:,.0f}".format(pos_fee_cap) }}.

Withdrawal: POS In = Total (full amount from customer's account, includes fee).

Deposit: POS Out = Amount (transferred to customer's account, excludes fee).

Carryover: Uses the day before the report start date.
